Donation Process

The donation process from the time you arrive at the center until the time you leave.


1.Registration
Registering on our blood donation website is a simple and impactful way to make a difference in the lives of those in need.

2.Health History
Your health history is an important part of ensuring safe and successful blood donation.

3.Donation
Donating blood is an act of compassion and generosity that can save lives.

4.Refreshment & Recovery
After your blood donation, we prioritize your comfort and well-being.

5.Save Life
When you donate blood, you become a hero in someone's life. Your act of kindness can mean the difference between life and death for patients
